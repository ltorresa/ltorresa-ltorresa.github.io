I am an avid off-pavement cyclist. I particularly enjoy bikepacking in the wilderness. Below you can find photo albums highlighting some of my cycling adventures (click on each photo cover to see more pictures).



  1. •Atlas Mountain Race Route: in March 2024, I rode solo most of the 800 miles of this epic race route in Morocco, from Marrakesh to Essaouira across the Atlas and the Anti-Atlas Mountains. My biggest cycling adventure so far.

 

            





  1. •New Mexico Off-Road Runner: 500 miles of dirt, gravel, and desert roads from Santa Fe to Las Cruces.

 

            





  1. •XVT bikepacking route (from Bridgewater to Stowe): a 4-day tour connecting singletrack trails along the spine of the Vermont Green Mountains.

 

            





  1. •Kokopelli trail: a 5-day, 140-mile bikepacking route from Fruita, CO to Moab, UT.


            





  1. •Fool’s loop: a 6-day, 230-mile bikepacking circuit starting from downtown Phoenix, AZ and going through Tonto National Forest, Agua Fria National Monument, and the Black Canyon.



              






  1. •Patagonia, Chile: a 4-day tour in Torres del Paine, Chile. Beautiful scenery and great single track riding on old horseback trails. Mind the strong winds (up to 40 miles/hour)!
