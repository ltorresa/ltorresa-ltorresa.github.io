Italy


  1. •Sellaronda Tour in the Italian Dolomites
    A full-day tour through four mountain passes (Gardena, Campolongo, Pordoi, Sella) accompanied by a professional alpine guide.


  
  




  1. •Northern Brenta, Trentino
    Solo ride through the Adamello-Brenta Nature Park from Malé to Cles via Passo Grosté. The descent from Passo Grosté to Lago di Tovel (via Malga Flavona and Malga Pozzol) is one of the most scenic downhill single tracks I have ever ridden. I did not encounter a single person during the 2.5 hour descent, just one marmot that I inadvertently scared to death.
